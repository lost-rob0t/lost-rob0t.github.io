* How to do multiple choice in yasnippet

Example from the blog
#+begin_src yasnippet
# -*- mode: snippet -*-
# name: test
# key: test
# --
Importance: ${1:$$(yas-choose-value '("high" "average" "below average"))}
Difficulty: ${2:$$(yas-choose-value '("easy" "hard"))}
$0
#+end_src
